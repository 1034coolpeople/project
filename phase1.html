Waking Up

You feel the world melting. Everything is shifting. You wake up, and all you see… Darkness. Shapes. Shadows.

A dim, flickering light. Your head pounds. The walls—wrong. Uneven. Where are you?

You're on the floor, confused.

You feel around in the darkness

You feel something cold, metallic.

It feels like a key.

You listen carefully

The voices grow louder.

You feel a growing sense of uneasiness.

You stand up

Your foot makes contact with a small object.

It sounds metallic.

You remain still

You feel a strong force pushing you up on to your feet, at the same time there's a loud knock coming from all around you.

You pick up the object

It's cold.

Suddenly it begins glowing a bright silver colour.

It looks like a key.
